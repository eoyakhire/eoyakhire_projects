Safety

Welcome to our vibrant community at Last Women on Earth! To ensure a positive and respectful environment for all members, we kindly ask everyone to abide by the following community guidelines:

1. Respect and Kindness: Treat fellow community members with respect and kindness, fostering a supportive atmosphere for open dialogue.
2. Inclusive Language: Use inclusive language and be mindful of diverse perspectives, ensuring everyone feels valued and included.
3. Original Content: Share your authentic voice and original content, avoiding plagiarism or the unauthorized use of others' work.
4. No Hate Speech: Refrain from any form of hate speech, discrimination, or harassment; we aim to create a space where everyone feels safe and welcome.
5. Privacy and Consent: Respect the privacy and consent of others, refraining from sharing personal information without explicit permission.
6. Constructive Criticism: Provide constructive feedback and engage in positive discussions, contributing to a supportive community for growth and learning.
7. Report Concerns: If you come across any content that violates these guidelines, please report it promptly to our moderation team for review.

By following these guidelines, you contribute to the creation of a vibrant and respectful community where everyone can freely express themselves. Thank you for being an essential part of Last Women on Earth's inclusive and creative space!
